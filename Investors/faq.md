# Investor FAQ

### Is Narrow a blockchain?
No. Narrow is a chain‑agnostic identity layer that works across blockchains and off‑chain systems.

### Does Narrow have a token?
No. Narrow is token‑less by design.

### What is the business model?
Ecosystem partnerships, enterprise integrations, and developer tooling.

### Why is this needed now?
AI agents, multi‑chain apps, and autonomous systems all require a shared identity foundation.
