# Narrow — Investor Materials

This folder contains all investor‑facing documents for Narrow Identity Layer Zero.  
The goal is to provide a clear, concise understanding of the market opportunity, the problem Narrow solves, and the long‑term vision of the protocol.

## Included Files

### 1. investor-summary.md  
A high‑level overview of Narrow for investors: problem, solution, market, and traction.

### 2. pitch-narrative.md  
A narrative version of the pitch deck story, explaining the strategic positioning of Narrow.

### 3. faq.md  
Answers to common investor questions about the protocol, business model, and roadmap.

- Investors  
- Strategic partners
